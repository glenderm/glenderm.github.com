New Patient Information

Identification

Please bring a photo ID (e.g. driver's license) AND proof of health insurance for your first visit. Without both, your appointment must be rescheduled.

Insurance

All three dermatologists are contracted providers for the following insurance companies:

  • Medicare
  • Blue Cross
  • Blue Shield
  • Cigna
  • Aetna
  • United Health Care
  • PHCS
  • Others

Patients who lack insurance and wish to pay in cash are accommodated with discounted prices.

Cancellation Policy

We require 24-hr notice to cancel your appointment. There is a $50 charge for missed appointments without prior notice.


Parking

There is free parking available at the lower levels of the office building. To reach our office, please take one of the elevators available at each parking level to the 4th floor.
